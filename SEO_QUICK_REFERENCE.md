## üö® Common SEO Issues to Avoid

### ‚ùå DON'T DO
- Don't keyword stuff (unnatural repetition)
- Don't hide text or links
- Don't use cloaking (show different content)
- Don't buy backlinks
- Don't copy content from other sites
- Don't block pages in robots.txt
- Don't use redirects to manipulate rankings

### ‚úÖ DO INSTEAD
- Use keywords naturally (1-2% density)
- Write for humans first, SEO second
- Build authentic backlinks
- Create original content
- Follow Google Webmaster Guidelines
- Keep site fast and mobile-friendly

## üìû Google Search Console Tips

### Verify Your Site in 3 Ways
1. **DNS Record** (Recommended) - Add TXT record
2. **HTML File** - Upload verification file
3. **Google Analytics** - If GA already linked

### Track These Metrics
- **Impressions**: People who see you in search
- **Clicks**: People who click your link
- **CTR**: Click-Through Rate (impressions/clicks)
- **Position**: Average ranking position

### Fix These Issues
- **Coverage errors**: Pages not indexed
- **Indexing issues**: robots.txt or noindex tags
- **Crawl errors**: Server errors preventing indexing
- **Mobile usability**: Mobile-specific problems
